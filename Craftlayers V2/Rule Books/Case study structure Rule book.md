CASE STUDY STRUCTURE RULEBOOK



Every case study must follow this structure:



1 Problem

2 Insight

3 Product Strategy

4 Antigravity Architecture

5 UX Design

6 AI Governance

7 Impact



Guidelines:



Problem

Describe the operational challenge.



Insight

Explain the key realization that reframed the problem.



Strategy

Describe the design direction chosen.



Architecture

Explain the decision engine and system logic.



UX Design

Show interface decisions and workflows.



AI Governance

Explain safeguards, transparency, and security.



Impact

Describe measurable or qualitative outcomes.



Avoid generic UX storytelling.



Focus on operational decision systems and product reasoning.


While drafting, sections may be written individually. However, only the final, merged case study version should be considered the official version. Once combined, the separate drafts should no longer be referenced.”
